# TravelSage AI — Explained for Grandma

Hi Grandma! Here's what I built, in plain words.

## What it is
TravelSage AI is a little website that helps people plan trips.

## How it works
1. You give it a travel guide — a PDF, a text file, or just notes you paste in.
2. The app reads your guide and remembers what's inside.
3. You can then ask it questions, and it will answer using **only** what your guide says — so it doesn't make things up.
4. With one click, it can also make you:
   - a day-by-day trip plan,
   - a packing list,
   - a simple budget,
   - and friendly tips about local customs and safety.

## Why it's special
Most travel chatbots guess. Mine is honest: if the answer isn't in your guide, it politely says "I don't know from your document" instead of inventing something. It also shows a little log so you can see what the AI was thinking.

## What's under the hood (in simple words)
- A **website** you open in your browser (the pretty part).
- A **helper program** behind the scenes that reads your guide, talks to an AI, and sends answers back.
- The AI is like a very well-read assistant — but I put it on a short leash so it sticks to your guide.

That's it. Upload a guide, ask questions, get a plan. Safe travels! ✈️
